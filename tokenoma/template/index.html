what is tokenoma?

tokenoma creative is an agency dedicated to helping businesses celebrate and recognize their teams' hard work. by partnering with companies, we deliver personalized appreciation through a subscription service. each friday, employees receive a thoughtfully curated gift box containing snacks, beverages, a small gift, and a heartfelt note recognizing their contributions. this simple yet meaningful gesture fosters team morale and strengthens workplace connections. with tokenoma creative, showing gratitude becomes effortless and impactful, making employees feel valued and appreciated every week.

our mission.

our mission is to foster a culture of appreciation and connection in workplaces. by partnering with businesses, we deliver thoughtfully curated gift boxes every friday, ensuring employees feel valued for their hard work and dedication. through this simple yet meaningful gesture, we aim to boost team morale, enhance employee satisfaction, and create stronger workplace bonds.

our vision.

our vision is to redefine workplace culture by becoming the go-to partner for businesses seeking to show gratitude to their teams. we strive to make appreciation a core value in every organization, creating a world where employees feel consistently recognized, motivated, and inspired to excel. through our innovative approach, we aim to leave a lasting impact on workplace relationships globally.
